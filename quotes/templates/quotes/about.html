About Mamoru Oshii

Mamoru Oshii is a famous Japanese director behind numerous philosophically-bent anime, such as Ghost in the Shell, Angel’s Egg, and Urusei Yatsura 2: Beautiful Dreamer. His works tend to draw heavily from the Bible and Christianity - such as the rebirth imagery in Angel’s Egg - and tend to rely heavily on surrealism and methodical pacing to communicate their ideas. Oshii’s work has been a huge influence on numerous Hollywood productions, such as The Matrix and Groundhog Day.
